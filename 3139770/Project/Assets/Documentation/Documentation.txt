To make use of this package:

-In this package we will make use of all the packages previously created and make a small game

-To make use of this package associate your knowledge of the previous packages

-When you play the game move around with the keys WASD

-Make sure to not bump into enemies and loose health otherwise the player will die and restart

-Use the left-click on the mouse to shoot and aim to the green cubes upon the player, every green cube hit is +1 point,
every red cube hit is -1 point on the score

-Try to make the best score without getting killed by the enemies and enjoy the game!
